Fit BioPac ≈ a_MLA·ML_A + b_MLA and BioPac ≈ a_EEGA·EEG_A + b_EEGA

Then EEG_A ≈ (a_MLA/a_EEGA)·ML_A + (b_MLA - b_EEGA)/a_EEGA (i.e., EEG_A = f_EEGA^{-1} ∘ f_MLA(ML_A))


Per-session constant baseline (simple & robust):
Estimate the lower envelope of the mark latencies and use that as the offset b. Ignore the big delays from queueing. This gives one affine map per session.

Slowly varying offset (if latency meanders over time):
Fit the drift (slope a) once, then model a small, smooth, time-varying offset δ(t) from the rolling lower quantile of residuals. Use E ≈ a·M + b + δ(t).
